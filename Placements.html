College Placements – 2025

Our college has a strong placement record and continues to prepare students for successful professional careers. In the academic year 2025, several students from our institution were successfully placed in Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), one of India’s leading multinational IT companies.

The placement in TCS reflects the quality of education, technical training, and soft skills development provided by our college. Through regular skill-development programs, workshops, and placement training sessions, students were well prepared to meet industry requirements.

We are proud of our students’ achievements and thankful to the placement cell and faculty members for their continuous guidance and support. These placements motivate future batches to work hard and achieve their career goals.
