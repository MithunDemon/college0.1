“Celebrating Tradition and Culture – Ethnic Day”

“Celebrating Culture with Our Respected Staff”

“Tradition and Elegance – Staff on Ethnic Day”

“Proud Moments of Our Staff on Ethnic Day”

Ethnic Day is a special and colorful celebration in our college that showcases the rich culture and traditions of India.

students and faculty members wear traditional ethnic dresses representing different states and communities.

The campus looks vibrant and joyful with a variety of costumes, colours, and cultural styles.

The event includes traditional dances, music, cultural performances, and fun activities. Students actively participate and show their talents while learning to respect and appreciate our cultural diversity.

Ethnic Day helps everyone feel connected to their roots and promotes unity among students.

Overall, Ethnic Day is one of the most memorable and enjoyable events in our college. It creates beautiful memories, strengthens friendships, and encourages cultural pride while spreading happiness and togetherness.
